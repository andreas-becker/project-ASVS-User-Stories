# V2.7 Out of Band Verifier

## ASVS: 2.7.1

## ASVS Requirement description

Verify that clear text out of band (NIST "restricted") authenticators,
such as SMS or PSTN, are not offered by default, and stronger
alternatives such as push notifications are offered first.
